Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our team directly.

Who is eligible to join a Nexus conference?

Our conferences are typically open to high school students (Grades 9-12) and university undergraduates. Specific eligibility may vary by event, so please check the individual conference page.

Do I need prior MUN experience?

Not at all! We welcome delegates of all skill levels. We offer beginner-friendly committees and provide extensive training materials, including workshops on rules of procedure and resolution writing.

What services do you offer besides conferences?

We provide a wide range of youth development programs, including public speaking workshops, leadership training bootcamps, CV building clinics, and personalized personality development coaching. Check our "Services" section for more details.

How can my school partner with BeWithNexus?

We are always excited to partner with educational institutions! We can organize custom workshops, training sessions for your school's MUN club, or provide special delegation packages for our conferences. Please contact us via the "Get Started" button to discuss a partnership.
